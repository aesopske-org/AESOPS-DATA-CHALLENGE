Project Overview This challenge ignites your passion for data visualization and economic analysis! Unleash the power of Gross County Product (GCP) data to unlock the economic potential of Kenya. By participating, you'll delve into the economic performance of individual counties and the sectors driving their growth. Your insights, presented through a compelling dashboard, will empower policymakers and local authorities to formulate data-driven strategies that bridge economic gaps and foster prosperity across Kenya.

The Challenge

This challenge presents an opportunity to conduct a comprehensive analysis of Kenya's Gross County Product (GCP) data. By examining historical trends and sectoral contributions across all 47 counties, participants will gain valuable insights into the nation's economic landscape. The ultimate goal is to create an informative and interactive dashboard that empowers policymakers with actionable recommendations to promote balanced economic growth throughout Kenya.

  • Growth Trends

    Analyze data to identify high-growth counties and uncover compelling economic narratives.

  • Sector Spotlight

    Explore economic sectors driving each county's GCP and assess their relative impact.

  • Understanding Disparities

    Investigate factors influencing economic disparities between counties and guide targeted development strategies.

  • Data-Driven Recommendations

    Formulate clear and actionable recommendations for policymakers based on your findings to promote balanced economic growth.
